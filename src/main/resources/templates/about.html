About Meter Notifier

Welcome to Meter Notifier, your one-stop solution for managing your energy consumption efficiently. We are dedicated to providing you with real-time insights into your meter details and helping you make informed decisions about your energy usage.


Our platform enables you to set personalized alerts, ensuring you never miss critical updates about your meter balance. With Meter Notifier, you can manage multiple meters from a single user account, making energy management convenient and hassle-free.


We believe that empowering our users with valuable energy insights can contribute to a greener and more sustainable future. Join our community today and take charge of your energy consumption!
